Babi Pongteh

Description

Babi pongteh is a much loved and traditional Peranakan dish containing tender cuts of pork stewed in a sweet and savoury fermented soy bean sauce.

Included in this dish are also potatoes, mushrooms and chestnuts soaking up all that delicious gravy. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Pork shoulder
  • Potatoes
  • Mushroom
  • Chestnut
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Fermented soya bean paste
  • Brown sugar
  • Dark soya sauce
  • Cinnamon Stick

Steps

  1. Blend shallots and garlic together into a fine paste.
  2. Sear pork belly and pork shoulder over high heat to give it a charred flavor
  3. Cook garlic and shallot paste over low heat till it becomes aromatic. Remove from heat
  4. Caramelise sugar over low heat till it turns into a syrup. Add in pork and shallot paste in and fry.
  5. Add dark soya sauce, fermented soya bean paste and cinnamon stick and mushroom and cook over a pressure cooker for about 30 min or till soft.
  6. Just before serving, add in precooked potatoes and chestnut and serve with a garnish of coriander.
