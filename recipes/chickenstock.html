Chicken Stock

Ingredients

  • Chicken bones and feet
  • Pork bones
  • Dried scallop
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Shallots
  • Coriander root
  • Crushed black peppercorn

Steps

  1. Boil chicken and pork bones over hot water and discard water to remove scum
  2. Readd the bones to the pot with all ingredients and cook over low heat till stock is ready.
